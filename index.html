I am a cofounder and developer of Copilot, which is a collection of data tools for Twitch as well as a place to collaborate and gain insights with other streamers. The web application consists of all the following features. Allows user to browse your chat reactivity in a simple and easy to use graph to see your best moments based on emotes and phrases used in chat.

It also allows you to scan your channel for accessibility issues, such as colorblindness colour schemes or patterns that may not suite your design. One if it's main features is allowing streamer to maintain their copyright standing within livestreams and creative content in a trackable dashboard.

Finally, you can do all of this collaboritively and invite other users, managers, or editors to be a part of your profile and collaborate on your Copiloted stream content.
